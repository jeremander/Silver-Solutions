About Us

Denise Silver


With over 25 years experience in Information Technology, Denise Silver has:

  • Served as Director of three PMOs in the insurance and utility company industries.
  • Implemented PMO startups from "soup to nuts."
  • Worked with PricewaterhouseCoopers and Deloitte Consulting to customize and implement Project Management methodologies.
  • Directed teams of project managers to implement 70+ IT projects.
  • Facilitated hundreds of meetings between stakeholders and IT to prioritize projects and define business requirements.
  • Created a multitude of persuasive PowerPoint presentations for corporate and academic settings.
  • Mentored and trained hundreds of staff and students on project management and technology topics.
  • Co-authored the definitive book on JAD meeting facilitation, Joint Application Development (John Wiley & Sons).
