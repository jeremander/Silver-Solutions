Slide Presentations

Avoid the pitfalls of PowerPoint

"PowerPoint is one of the most abused presentation tools in use today." — almost everyone

There are approximately 500 million PowerPoint users in the world. They do 35 million presentations each day.

About a million presentations are going on right now — 50% of them are unbearable.

The average PowerPoint slide length is 40 words, bloated with bullets, drowning in text, and displaying complicated charts that are impossible to read from the back of the room. Presenters often digress from goals, overuse animation, and ultimately lose their audience.

Presentations bring in over $100B in sales annually, so, what can you do to avoid this torture by PowerPoint and create slides that work?
