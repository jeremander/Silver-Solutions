How We Work with You

1. Strategize

  • Kick off a project via phone or Zoom.
  • Discuss presentation objectives, target audience, brand identity, and timeline.
  • Brainstorm content ideas and agree on deliverables.

2. Design and Build

  • Share slide mockups to finalize creative direction. Agree on preferred "look and feel."
  • Create a first draft presentation for review.

3. Finalize

  • Collaborate to edit/tweak the content.
  • Work until you are totally satisfied with the results.

4. Rehearse

  • As an option, rehearse the presentation together.
  • Receive coaching to create stronger impact and relieve stress.
