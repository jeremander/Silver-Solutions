About Us

Denise Silver

PowerPoint Specialist and
Résumé Coach

Going out on my own! With 25 years of experience in IT, project management, marketing, training, and staffing, I joined the gig economy. This offers freedom to accept interesting work in corporate, academic, and social settings.

A graduate of Muhlenberg College and George Washington University with degrees in Spanish, I was headed for a career in teaching―but computer programming grabbed me first. Yet my Spanish has come in handy with many corporate requests for translations.

My career spans diverse industries, such as home health care, water utilities, insurance, and academia. Along the way, I showed a knack for recruiting talented people, and hired hundreds of them. I read thousands of resumes and know how to create a winning one.

Over several decades, I facilitated hundreds of meetings to capture information, define business processes, and design websites. I even co-authored a book on how to do this. I created and delivered PowerPoint presentations that helped people market their products, inspire their audience, and tell their story. Now I’m ready to work with you on your next presentation!

What motivates me when I’m not creating presentations? I love playing tennis, capturing photos (with a real camera), frequenting neighborhood cappuccino bars, and accompanying my singing children on the piano.

If you’re looking for a creative, collaborative communicator who can help you design presentations that satisfy your goals to persuade, inform and entertain―I’m your partner.
