How We Work with You

1. Getting to Know You

Kick off a project with a phone (or Zoom) call to discuss your job history and what you are looking for in your next job.

2. Create Résumé

Use a template chosen from our library to create a new résumé. Collaborate to fine-tune it—including a summary profile that distinguishes you from the competition. Tweak the résumé together until satisfied with the results.

3. Find Potential Jobs

Share potential jobs from the popular websites. Revisit the résumé and update accordingly.

4. Prep for the Interview

Simulate the interview experience with a mock interview and critiques along the way.
